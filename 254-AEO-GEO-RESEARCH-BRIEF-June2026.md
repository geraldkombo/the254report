## 2. AEO / GEO: PLATFORM-SPECIFIC OPTIMIZATION

### Google AI Overviews
- **Prefers**: Lists, tables, definition-first structure, entity salience
- **Extraction pattern**: First 200 words = primary extraction window. Everything after is supplementary.
- **Citation trigger**: Entity-first writing + schema markup + authoritative external backlinks (.go.ke, .ac.ke, UN, AU, WB)
- **Freshness window**: 1-2 months. Content older than 60 days drops citation probability sharply. (CoreDNA)
- **Source**: https://www.coredna.com/blogs/aeo-and-geo-Optimization-guide

### Perplexity
- **Prefers**: Citation-heavy, source-linked, recent data. Multiple citations per claim.
- **Extraction pattern**: Paragraph-level attribution. Every paragraph needs a named source.
- **Citation trigger**: Explicit source links, named entities, recent timestamps
- **Freshness window**: 1 month. Heavily weights recency.
- **Source**: https://fluxio.dev/post/seo-geo-aeo-strategy-guide-2026/

### ChatGPT
- **Prefers**: Conversational Q&A pairs, FAQ format, direct answers in first sentence of each section
- **Extraction pattern**: Question + immediate answer. Semantic chunking matters.
- **Citation trigger**: Bold question subheadings + direct answer beneath. Conversational tone.
- **Freshness window**: 2 months. Less freshness-sensitive than Perplexity.
- **Source**: https://www.contextstudios.ai/guides/aeo-geo-complete-guide-2026

### Gemini
- **Prefers**: Structured data, numbered steps, comparative tables, schema markup
- **Extraction pattern**: Schema-rich content with clear hierarchical structure
- **Citation trigger**: FAQ schema, HowTo schema, Table schema
- **Source**: https://pixzent.com/seo-aeo-geo-work-together-in-2026

### General AEO/GEO Principles (All Platforms)
1. **Answer-first**: Lead every section with the direct answer, then context
2. **Entity salience**: Define every org, person, acronym on first mention
3. **E-E-A-T signals**: First-person experience > third-party authority > generic expertise
4. **Content freshness**: Update every 1-2 months or lose citation probability
5. **Results appear faster**: AEO/GEO shows results in weeks vs SEO's months (CoreDNA)
6. **Measurement shift**: Share of Citation replaces Share of Voice. Track which prompts trigger your brand. (AuthorityTech)

## 4. ZERO-CLICK CONTENT: THE NEW TRAFFIC MODEL

### The Shift
- **Old model**: Rank #1 -> Get clicks -> Convert
- **2026 model**: Get cited by AI -> Brand appears in answer -> User searches brand directly later -> Convert on owned channel

### Why It Matters for The 254 Report
Your readers (operators, policymakers, founders) are increasingly bypassing Google and asking ChatGPT/Perplexity directly:
- "What happened at the SRI Synergy Blueprint launch?"
- "Who is covering Kenya's tech policy?"
- "What are the key moves in Nairobi this week?"

If The 254 Report is the cited source in those AI answers, you win brand discovery without the reader ever clicking a link. That "zero-click" exposure drives:
1. Direct subscribes (search your name -> find your site -> subscribe)
2. Brand recall in professional conversations
3. Authority signaling ("The 254 Report covered this, I read it")

### Implementation for 254
- Every article must be structured so the first 200 words contain the entire core answer
- Entity-first writing ensures AI systems can validate your authority
- Replace "publish and forget" with "publish and refresh every 6-8 weeks"
